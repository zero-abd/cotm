About COTM

Castaway On The Moon

Castaway On The Moon also known as COTM, the most promising, versatile and distinguished online art group began its journey on 12th April, 2020, to promote the zeal of art, creativity and imagination. The mind is the limit when it comes to art and we truly believe in that motto.

Our Journey

Castaway On The Moon is a gigantic platform consisting currently of almost half a million regular members. Our primary incentive is to give every single creative mind in the country an opportunity to show off their art, nurture their skills, connect, learn and grow as artists, pioneers of their own styles.

We started off very small but now we are one of the largest networks of artists in the country who regularly post and collaborate with each other on a daily basis in our Facebook group.
